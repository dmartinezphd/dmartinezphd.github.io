Further Study Required

Do a google scholar search with keywords "further study required." You'll find thousands upon thousands of papers that use that phrase or a close variation. Here's just a very small sampling of ones that I found in my most recent search:


"...require further study." 

"Further study will be required,
however,"

"...further study should be pursued in this area."

"further study of the basic data is in order."

"Further study required" is a catch-all phrase, which opens the door for continuing investigation in that particular field. It's basically saying, "this is what we got and we think it's meaningful but we're not sure so someone, maybe us, need to keep working on this to confirm, refute, or further illuminate the cool and/or bizarre stuff we just discovered. 

The phrase is a hallmark of what science is all about.
